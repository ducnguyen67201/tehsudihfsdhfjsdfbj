# GTM

This is the active go-to-market workspace for TrustLoop's first users.

## Current Wedge

- User: founder-CEO at a `5-15` person B2B SaaS company
- Current workflow: handles customer support in Slack
- Pain: becomes the manual support router and loses focus
- First motion: concierge discovery + targeted founder outreach

## Files

- `icp-and-positioning.md` — who we are targeting and why
- `sourcing-playbook.md` — where to find leads and how to qualify them
- `lead-scoring-rubric.md` — how to score whether a founder is worth messaging
- `outreach-copy.md` — DM templates and follow-up copy
- `daily-workflow.md` — what to do each day if you are new to GTM
- `STATUS.md` — current weekly priorities, targets, and open questions
- `call-notes-template.md` — structure for discovery calls
- `learnings.md` — what we are hearing and how the thesis changes

## Companion Skill

- `.skills/gtm-founder-outreach/` — reusable sourcing + scoring + DM workflow for
  founder outreach tasks

## Working Rules

- Narrow beats broad
- Real quotes beat opinions
- Ten sharp leads beat one hundred vague leads
- Update this folder after calls, not just after product work
- Confusion is normal. Default to talking to real founders instead of trying to
  invent a perfect growth system up front.
